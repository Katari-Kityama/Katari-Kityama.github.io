Reading

I don't read as much as I used to, but I couldn't resist reading Murtagh when Malachi offered me an opportunity to borrow the book.

Reading is a good way to practice focusing. When you read a book, you commit to a single task for an extended period of time afterall. This allows you to improve your ability to focus on a single task at a time in an enjoyable way, which is just one reason why reading is a good habit.
