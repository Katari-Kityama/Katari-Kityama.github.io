Routers

I bought some cheap used routers to enhance my internet and make my VR setup wireless.

Router 1 is the home internet router and is connected to router 2 wirelessly. Connected to router 2 is my computer and router 3, providing internet access to my computer and allows connections from router 3 to pass to the computer. Router 3 is connected to the VR headset and its trackers, and sends this information to router 2 and then the computer.
